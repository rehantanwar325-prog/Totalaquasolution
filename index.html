Frequently Asked Questions

Water purifier service aur purchase se jude aam sawal-jawab.

Water purifier filter kab change karwana chahiye?

Aamtaur par, sediments aur carbon filters ko har 6 se 12 mahine me change karna chahiye. Agar aapke yahan pani me impurity zyada hai ya pani ka flow kam ho jata hai, to filter jaldi badalna pad sakta hai.

RO membrane ki life kitni hoti hai?

Ek standard RO Membrane ki life lagbhag 1 se 2 saal tak hoti hai. Yeh is baat par depend karta hai ki aapke ghar aane wale pani ka TDS kya hai aur pani ka total usage kitna hai.

Kya service ke bad spares/parts pe warranty milti hai?

Haan, humare dwara install kiye gaye genuine parts aur membranes par hum 3 se 6 mahine ki replacement warranty dete hain. Details ke liye service technician se sampark karein.

Kya service charges fix hote hain?

Humare normal inspection and visiting charges ₹199 hain. Uske baad jo bhi parts replace honge, unka cost extra hoga. Hum repair se pehle aapko estimation batate hain.
